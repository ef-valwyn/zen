Our Story

A focused reboot: design leadership, outsourced excellence

Zen Yachts (Zen2) is entering a new chapter marked by commercial focus, technical discipline, and operational agility. We operate a lean, IP‑led model—outsourcing day‑to‑day build and engineering to an established manufacturer in Thailand while concentrating on design, owner experience, and the expansion of our flagship ZeroLap™ programme.

The strategy is deliberately tight and profitable: limited production, disciplined scarcity, and a single, premium model—the ZEN 55 OGI. From year two, we plan to maintain one stock boat for market agility, with production scaled to confirmed demand.

  • Lean corporate centre in Singapore/Thailand
  • Outsourced build & engineering partnership
  • One model, one uncompromising standard
  • Scarcity by design to sustain value

Reboot Highlights

ZEN50 sea trials commence in Thailand. Several ZEN55 OGI builds are slated for 2026–2027 delivery. ZeroLap integrates with global rally schedules to validate ocean performance and build a trusted owner community.

ZEN 55 OGI

Sanctuary yacht. Infinite range. Category A confidence.

A single, uncompromising specification: a 55‑foot, Category A ocean‑going catamaran (four cabins + two crew) derived from a proven platform and optimised with extended sugar scoops. Solar‑electric propulsion pairs with an ocean‑proven wingsail for extraordinary range and energy independence.

Redundant by design

Dual watermakers, mirrored electrical architecture, independent comms (Starlink + Iridium), and comprehensive lightning resilience.

Luxury, kept light

Minimalist‑weight interior finishes for comfort without compromising performance; engineered for real ocean readiness.

Scarcity creates value

Low‑volume, high‑discipline production designed to sustain premium positioning and resale strength over time.

Price guidance

Target positioning at ~USD 3.0M per unit (initially from ~USD 2.8M) under a constrained production model.

ZeroLap™

The ocean, on your terms — supported circumnavigation

ZeroLap is our experiential platform, inspired by long‑range rallies and delivered with established organisers. Owners can join full or partial circumnavigation legs with route planning, technical support, spares provisioning, onboard engineering advice, and crew/training assistance.

Hull 1 (ZEN50) and the first ZEN55 OGI are slated to join in late 2026 from the Indo‑Pacific, building credibility, community, and real ocean miles from day one.

  • World‑scale route planning & logistics
  • Technical & operational support
  • Owner community and shared knowledge
  • Co‑marketing with cross‑linking for SEO/AEO
